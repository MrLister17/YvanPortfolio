# Privacy review

- Only local demonstration GMA interface screenshots were copied to the portfolio.
- Private practicum records, daily time records, signatures, internal links, customer information, proprietary documents, private prompts, and regulatory findings were not copied.
- The practicum narrative is intentionally general and includes the academic-records note requested in the brief.
- The GMA case study is labelled as a prototype and does not claim legal authority, production adoption, company endorsement, or verified business impact.
- Fitness Buddy avoids medical outcomes, medical-device claims, marketplace availability, unsupported metrics, downloads, and public repository claims.
- No analytics, contact-form data collection, database, secret, or runtime AI dependency was added.
